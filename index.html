THE BEGINNING OF US !!!

I honestly didn’t know anyone was watching me that day. However, my first impression of Doro was: Who really is this guy?!

It was the second day of the I-Soar conference. I arrived late to the service but danced and rejoiced my way into the canopy. The weather suddenly changed, and I slipped in quickly to be safe.

There stood a guy separate from the rest of us he was standing under the changing weather, soon the rain came in heavily and the guy still stood there, I was scooting to the middle of the canopy because even been at the edge I wasn't safe.There stood a guy separate from the rest of us he was standing gallant under the changing weather, soon the rain came in heavily and the guy still stood there, I was scooting to the middle of the canopy because even the edge where I stood wasn't safe.

Someone came to tap me asked me to help keep his phone away from the rain which I did, I kept it neatly tucked in my handbag , later doro told me that when he saw me dancing into the service his first thoughts were "I'd like to be with someone like this".

We didn’t cross paths again until the end of the service. Suddenly, Doro remembered his phone wasn’t with him. He started looking around for the girl he had handed it to, I walked up to him and returned it because I was watching him look for me. I still remember his gratitude; he kept repeating, “Thank you so much, thank you so much.” That was the first time he truly saw me, and as he would later confess, I never left his heart after that moment.

Two months passed before Doro decided to speak to me. He saw me during an invasion evangelism, and he always jokes that I was dressed in white on white that day. Seeing me again stirred something in him, and he sought counsel from a friend, confessing that he liked me and wanted to talk to me. His friend encouraged him to start a conversation, but Doro said he felt strongly in his spirit to first speak to my pastor about his intentions for marriage.

The following week, after an evening meeting, he saw my pastor. While waiting, he spotted me at a distance, quickly took a picture, and showed it to my pastor because he didn’t even know my name.

That same day, my pastor called me. He said, “A young man came to me today. He said he likes you and wants to marry you.” I laughed and told him I’d stop by on Tuesday so he could show me the picture of the young man. From Friday to Tuesday, my mind ran through a hundred possible subjects, and Doro wasn’t even on the list!

When I finally saw the picture and realized it was him, I felt immense gratitude to God. My heart accepted him instantly. I asked my pastor to send my number to him.

The next day, he called, and we agreed to meet. I visited his shop to see the man I was about to love wholeheartedly in his own space. We sat and talked for an hour. He spoke with so much sincerity about why he wanted to be with me.

And that, right there, is how we met. The rest, as they say, is history but for us, it’s more than history. It’s a love story written by God Himself.
